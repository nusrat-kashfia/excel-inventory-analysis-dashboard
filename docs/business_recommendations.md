# Business Recommendations

## Project Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze inventory, sales, warehouse, and purchase order data using Microsoft Excel to support inventory planning and operational decision-making.

The dashboard was built using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, XLOOKUP, KPI cards, and slicers to provide an interactive view of business performance.

---

# Executive Summary

The dashboard provides an overview of key operational metrics including sales performance, inventory value, warehouse performance, supplier delivery performance, and stock status.

The analysis helps identify trends that support better inventory management and purchasing decisions.

---

# Key Findings

## 1. Sales Performance

Sales revenue is generated across multiple product categories, with some categories contributing significantly more than others.

### Recommendation

- Prioritize inventory planning for high-performing product categories.
- Allocate purchasing budgets based on historical sales performance.

---

## 2. Warehouse Performance

Warehouse sales performance varies across different warehouse locations.

### Recommendation

- Monitor warehouse performance regularly.
- Investigate lower-performing warehouses to identify operational improvements.
- Balance inventory distribution according to regional demand.

---

## 3. Inventory Value

Inventory value differs across product categories.

### Recommendation

- Closely monitor high-value inventory.
- Reduce excess inventory where demand is relatively low.
- Review purchasing quantities regularly to minimize inventory holding costs.

---

## 4. Stock Status

The stock status summary indicates that some products are below their reorder point.

### Recommendation

- Replenish products below their reorder point as a priority.
- Review reorder levels periodically to ensure adequate inventory availability.

---

## 5. Top Revenue Products

A relatively small number of products contribute a large share of total revenue.

### Recommendation

- Maintain sufficient inventory for top-selling products.
- Monitor demand trends to avoid stock-outs.

---

## 6. Monthly Sales Trend

Monthly sales remain relatively stable over the selected period.

### Recommendation

- Continue monitoring monthly sales trends.
- Use historical sales patterns to improve inventory forecasting.

---

## 7. Supplier Delivery Performance

Supplier delivery performance varies between suppliers.

### Recommendation

- Monitor supplier performance regularly.
- Review suppliers with consistently poor delivery performance.
- Include delivery performance as part of supplier evaluation.

---

# Business Value

This dashboard enables decision-makers to:

- Monitor inventory performance
- Track sales trends
- Evaluate warehouse performance
- Review supplier delivery performance
- Identify products requiring replenishment
- Support purchasing decisions with operational data

---

# Conclusion

The dashboard demonstrates how Microsoft Excel can be used to transform large operational datasets into meaningful business insights.

By combining Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI reporting, XLOOKUP, and interactive slicers, the dashboard provides a practical solution for monitoring inventory performance, warehouse operations, supplier performance, and sales activity.

The analysis supports informed business decisions and helps improve inventory planning and operational efficiency.
